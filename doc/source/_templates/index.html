Overview

The scikits.hydroclimpy package is a loose collection of utilities to manipulate hydro-climatologic data, such as series of temperatures, precipitation, streamflow, evapotranspiration... Climate information can easily be associated to a series to take the impact of climate variability into account.

This package was originally designed as a series of extensions to NumPy. With time, part of the code was incorporated in NumPy as the numpy.ma module to handle missing data, and in the scikits.timeseries scikit.

The scikits.hydroclimpy module provides classes and functions for manipulating, reporting, and plotting climate time series of various frequencies (daily, monthly, seasonal...). The focus is on convenient data access and manipulation while leveraging the existing mathematical functionality in numpy and scipy.
